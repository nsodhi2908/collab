Charlotte's Web Design

Fleming College's Geographic Information Systems (GIS) Specialist program includes specializations in applications and cartography. Students in this Ontario Post-Graduate Certificate Program are able to use high-end technology to solve real world problems. Group 7 students have created a consulting team tailored the industry using innovation. The team in Charlotte's Web can come up with unique solutions tailored to your needs. Whether it is in the domain of programming, mapping or database design, we can help.

Program Summary

This program will engage students in the exploration of the cartographic communication process. Effective geographical display is dependent upon the decisions involved in its design, such as colour, symbology, and typography in map making. Students will also be introduced to concepts and processes that are central to cartography, enabling the student to build a cartographic foundation for subsequent studies. Industry standard, graphic design software will be used to create various paper products and screen images.
