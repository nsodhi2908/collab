The Client

For this project it was our objective to make an impact on the food security problem of the Kawartha Lakes and Haliburton Region. The Kawartha Lakes Food Coalition has requested AlphaProductions to help the public and increase food security by producing a web application that displays the locations and information on various Fruit Trees within the Kawartha Lakes and Haliburton Region.

The Problem

To complete this task we created a form to submit georeferenced data on the locations of trees and the ability to share and display them on either a mobile or desktop web browser!
